Jungleverse is a multiplayer, play-to-earn game launching on the Ethereum blockchain. You can battle, breed, and earn $JUNGLE with your Jungleverse characters, also known as Junglees. There are 30 gen-1 species of Junglees that currently live in the Jungleverse. Each Junglee is a programmatically generated NFT and only a total of 10,000 of them will ever be up for adoption.
