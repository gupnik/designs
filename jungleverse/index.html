Welcome to the
Jungleverse is a multiplayer, play-to-earn game launching on the Ethereum blockchain. You can battle, breed, and earn $JUNGLE with your Jungleverse characters, also known as Junglees.There are 30 gen-1 species of Junglees that currently live in the Jungleverse. Each Junglee is a programmatically generated NFT and only a total of 10,000 of them will ever be up for adoption.
#NFT

ERC 721

Each Junglee is an ERC 721 token that is randomly generated from over 300k possible combinations.

#NFT

ERC 20

With every Junglee you adopt, you will receive 500 $JUNGLE tokens, the in-game ERC20 currency that accrues 100% APR (Upto a year).

What can I do with
Naming your Junglee

Give your Junglee a unique name and identity. Your character’s name will be stored and publicly visible on the Ethereum Blockchain. This identity can be changed anytime later in the future for free. To change the name of your Junglee, you will need to spend 150 $JUNGLE.

Breed

There are 30 species of Junglees that currently live in the Jungleverse. You can use $JUNGLE to breed two different Gen-1 species of Junglees to create a new hybrid species. This newly created Gen-2 species of Junglee will have characteristics of both its parents.

Battle

Jungleverse will be an ever expanding world where you can use your Junglee to battle other Junglees or fight menacing robots. Level up your Junglee to earn $JUNGLE.

Claim Future Drops

Owning a Junglee gives you exclusive access to claim future Jungleverse drops free of charge.The first of which will be weapons and companions that will hold utility in the game.
