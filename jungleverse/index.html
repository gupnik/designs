Welcome to the
Jungleverse is a multiplayer, play-to-earn game launching on the Ethereum blockchain. You can battle, breed, and earn $JUNGLE with your Jungleverse characters, also known as Junglees.There are 30 gen-1 species of Junglees that currently live in the Jungleverse. Each Junglee is a programmatically generated NFT and only a total of 10,000 of them will ever be up for adoption.

ERC 721

Each Junglee is an ERC 721 token that is randomly generated from over 300k possible combinations.

ERC-721 Junglees

  1. What are Junglees?

    $Junglees are a collection of 10,000 ERC 721 tokens that are stored on the Ethereum Blockchain. You can use your Junglee to battle, breed and earn $JUNGLE tokens in the Jungleverse game.

  2. How do I adopt a Junglee?

    You can adopt your Junglee from our website. Once all 10,000 gen-1 characters are adopted, you can get them from the secondary market on opensea.

  3. How many species of Junglees are there?

    There are 30 species of Junglees that currently roam the Jungleverse.

  4. Will the team create and sell new Junglees after the initial sale?

    No, the team will not offer any new Junglees after the initial sale. The only way to create new ones is by breeding them. Holders of Gen-1 Junglees will be able to breed and create new hybrid species and thereby define the future of the Jungleverse.

ERC 20

With every Junglee you adopt, you will receive 500 $JUNGLE tokens, the in-game ERC20 currency that accrues 100% APR (Upto a year).

ERC 20 $JUNGLE

  1. What is $JUNGLE?

    $Jungle is an ERC-20 token that can be used as currency in the Jungleverse.

  2. What can I do with $JUNGLE?

    Initially, you can use it to change the name of your Junglee and give it a unique identity. Once the game is launched, you can use $JUNGLE to breed your Junglees, forge weapons and buy in-game items.

  3. How much $JUNGLE do I get when I adopt a Junglee?

    You will receive 500 $JUNGLE tokens/ Junglee. This accrues 100% APR (Upto a year)

  4. What happens if I sell my Jungleverse NFT during the 1 year $JUNGLE accrual period?

    If/when you sell your Junglee before the 1 year period is complete, the new owner of the NFT will be entitled to receive the pending $JUNGLE tokens from the date of sale.

  5. How can I get more $JUNGLE?

    We do not offer an exchange or marketplace for $JUNGLE. Currently, the only way to get more $JUNGLE is to transfer it from another wallet that holds the token. Once the game launches, you can earn $JUNGLE by playing the game and completing in-game missions.

  6. What is the tokenomics behind $JUNGLE?

    When it comes to tokenomics, the number of $JUNGLE in circulation is rather fluid and does not have a hard cap. This is because the tokens are burned whenever new Junglees are bred.

  7. What is the current supply of $JUNGLE?

    At launch, there will be a supply of 11,000,000 $JUNGLE tokens.

    • 5,000,000 distributed to the public at the mint
    • 500,000 for the Jungleverse Team at launch
    • 5,000,000 generated by Jungleverse NFTs over 1 year
    • 500,000 for the Jungleverse team over 1 year

    $JUNGLE allocated to the team will be used at the discretion of the team. This may be used to provide liquidity and giveaways among other things.

Naming your Junglee

Give your Junglee a unique name and identity. Your character’s name will be stored and publicly visible on the Ethereum Blockchain. This identity can be changed anytime later in the future for free. To change the name of your Junglee, you will need to spend 150 $JUNGLE.

Breed

There are 30 species of Junglees that currently live in the Jungleverse. You can use $JUNGLE to breed two different Gen-1 species of Junglees to create a new hybrid species. This newly created Gen-2 species of Junglee will have characteristics of both its parents.

Battle

Jungleverse will be an ever expanding world where you can use your Junglee to battle other Junglees or fight menacing robots. Level up your Junglee to earn $JUNGLE.

Claim Future Drops

Owning a Junglee gives you exclusive access to claim future Jungleverse drops free of charge.The first of which will be weapons and companions that will hold utility in the game.
